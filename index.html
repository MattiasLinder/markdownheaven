Why markdown?

"Human-readable text is the language of AI communication."

When you write a prompt, you write in human-readable text. When AI responds, it responds in human-readable text — most often in markdown. Markdown is structured enough to carry meaning: headings, lists, emphasis, code, tables. And transparent enough that you never lose the words underneath. This is not a coincidence — plain text is what language models are built on. The problem is that the rest of the software world hasn't caught up. That's the gap these tools fill.
