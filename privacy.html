What this site does

markdownheaven.com is an informational site — a hub for markdown tools and guides. It does not process any documents, text, or personal content. Nothing you type or upload anywhere is sent to our servers, because there is no such functionality here.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how the site is used — which pages are visited, roughly where visitors come from, and what devices they use. GA4 collects anonymised data only. No personal information linked to your content is ever collected.

IP addresses are anonymised before being logged. You can opt out at any time via the cookie banner at the bottom of the page, or by using the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

Cookies

We use cookies solely for GA4 analytics. No advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party marketing tools are used on this site.

Third-party links

This site links to external products (markdownword.com, showmarkdown.com, promptcorrector.com) and to Google Fonts and CDN resources. Each of those services has its own privacy policy.
